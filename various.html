An interactive business process map

Background

While working as a Product Designer at Fannie Mae, I was tasked with designing and developing an interactive business process. Details of the process are confidential and have been removed, but the design and development process is described here.

The purpose of the interactive business process was to simplify the experience of onboarding new employees with their respective tasks and responsibilities relative to Fannie Mae as a whole. The interactive site allowed employees to view each step in the process, and to provide feedback on its details as procedures changed and improved over time.

I designed the visuals in Adobe Illustrator, and then embedded the SVG markup into the HTML of the site. I then use a combination of CSS animation, JQuery code, and JSON text files to prototype the interactivity.

Website mockups

Background

While working on a short-term contract as part of a startup in its very early stages, I was part of a design team working on different features of a small business site & app: Servpal. Servpal was intended to help small business owners connect with clients, and allow clients to book their services.

My tasks included designing a concept landing page for the website, as well as initial concepts for the services booking flow. I spoke with the CEO, and some small business owners who were part of the team, to begin to get an idea of what their needs were.
