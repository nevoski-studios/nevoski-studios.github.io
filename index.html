About me

Here are just some of the skills I employ to translate each aspect of a design challenge into user-centered solutions.

UI Design

From low to high fidelity, I use Figma, Sketch, and other tools to create detailed components, design systems, and mockups, iterating based on feedback.

UX Research

From interviews and journey mapping to competitive analyses, I learn about the design challenge to make sure every potential pain point is addressed.

Prototyping

From simple paper prototypes to fully interactive user flows using Figma and HTML/CSS, I create interactive prototypes for each level of fidelity as needed.

Usability Testing

Using mockups interactive prototypes, I build meaningful test scenarios designed to find key pain points and empathize with user experiences and needs.

Illustration

I have experience as an amateur illustrator, and I enjoy creating icons, logos, animations, and marketing materials, all in the browser-friendly SVG format.

Development

My experience as a front-end developer, and familiarity with coding concepts and development practices, makes it easy for me to communicate with dev teams.
